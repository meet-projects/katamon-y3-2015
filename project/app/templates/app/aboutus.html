Our Team

Our team consists of 8 members; Aseel, Itay, Leen, Mouhammad, Shir, Yael, Wassim and Shadi. We're a group of 16-17 year olds, participating in the MEET program.

VolunTeam was founded after we found that many people who want to volunteer face difficulties finding institutions and organizations to volunteer in. We offers a solution to this problem, by offering volunteering opportunities in a range of different fields and connecting between volunteers and organisations.
